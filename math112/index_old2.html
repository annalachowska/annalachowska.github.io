Course Description: The objective of Math 112 is to introduce the notions of derivative and of definite integral for functions of one variable, with some of their physical and geometrical motivation and interpretations. Emphasis will be placed on acquiring an understanding of the concepts that underlie the subject, and on the use of those concepts in problem solving. Mathematica exercises will be used for some explorations.


Text: The text is James Stewart's Single Variable Calculus: Early Transcendentals. Math 112, seventh edition, Brooks/Cole 2012, 2008.


Evaluation: Course grades are determined by homework (10%), the two midterm exams (25% each), and the final exam (40%). Calculators may not be used on the exams or on the final.
